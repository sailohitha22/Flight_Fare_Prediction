# Airline Ticket Price Prediction using Data Analytics

## Problem Statement
Airline ticket prices are highly dynamic and influenced by multiple factors such as travel seasonality, departure schedules, demandâ€“supply trends, flight routes, seat availability, and airline operational costs. These continuous fluctuations make it challenging to accurately forecast ticket prices or provide reliable fare estimates to customers in advance.

---

## Research Objective
This project aims to explore how **data analytics, predictive modeling, and IoT-enabled data streams** can be utilized to forecast airline ticket prices more accurately. The study focuses on identifying key drivers of fare variation, evaluating suitable machine learning models, and assessing how predictive insights can support strategic decision-making in the airline industry.

---

## Data Utilization & Feature Engineering
- Analysis of **historical flight and booking data**, including:
  - Airline, route, departure time, duration, and number of stops  
  - Seasonal travel patterns and demand indicators  
  - Seat class and booking timelines  
- Feature engineering to identify **high-impact variables** influencing price fluctuations.
- **IoT integration perspective**:
  - Use of real-time data from smart airport systems, passenger flow sensors, and reservation platforms.
  - Incorporation of IoT-driven demand signals such as occupancy trends and check-in activity to enhance predictive accuracy.

---

## Modeling Approaches
- Evaluation of multiple predictive techniques:
  - Linear and multivariate regression models  
  - Time-series forecasting methods  
  - Machine learning algorithms such as Gradient Boosting and ensemble models  
- Comparative analysis based on:
  - Prediction accuracy  
  - Model interpretability  
  - Scalability in real-world airline pricing systems  

---

## Business & Operational Impact
- Enables **dynamic pricing strategies** and improved revenue management.
- Supports **inventory optimization** and efficient seat allocation.
- Enhances **customer experience** through:
  - Fare alerts  
  - Data-driven booking recommendations  
- Demonstrates how **IoT-integrated predictive systems** can be embedded into airline decision-support frameworks for strategic planning and demand forecasting.

---

## Expected Outcomes
- Deeper understanding of airline fare behavior using data-driven and IoT-enhanced models.
- Improved accuracy in airline ticket price estimation.
- Measurable business value in terms of:
  - Pricing efficiency  
  - Customer satisfaction  
  - Optimized operational planning in the airline and travel ecosystem
